about us

In October 2015 the Makerere University Private sector Forum unveiled plans to build the Emmanuel Tumusiime-Mutebile Centre of Excellence (TMCE). The Mutebile Centre, which will be allocated AT Makerere University, honors Dr. Mutebile, Governor of the Bank of Uganda and a longstanding champion of private sector development in Uganda.
