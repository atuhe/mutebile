TMCE Mandate & Objectives

TMCE Mandate

The original mandate of the MUPSF was threefold:

• To build knowledge transfer partnerships with the public and private sectors
• To increase public/private sector participation in the university affairs
• To promote private sector competitiveness and value addition to university products

Specific Objectives

1. To encourage private sector to participate in university activities
2. To lobby and advocate for policies in the interest of all stakeholders
3. To promote the development of demand driven skills at the university
4. To enhance research, technology development and transfer for the private sector needs
5. To foster a strong and sustainable collaboration and networking in the region
